3. Dubai trip
17/01/2025
Day 1: Arrival & Exploring Downtown Dubai Morning: Arrive in Dubai & check into your hotel. Visit Dubai Mall (one of the world's largest malls). Explore Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo inside the mall. Afternoon: Lunch at Social House (international cuisine with Burj Khalifa views). Visit the Burj Khalifa (At the Top, Level 124 & 125 or Level 148 for VIP experience). Explore Souk Al Bahar for shopping and traditional vibes. Evening: Watch the Dubai Fountain Show (every 30 minutes from 6 PM). Dinner at Armani Amal (luxury Indian dining at Burj Khalifa). Day 2: Old Dubai & Desert Safari Morning: Visit Al Fahidi Historical District (Dubai’s old town, museums, and art galleries). Explore Dubai Museum inside Al Fahidi Fort. Cross the Dubai Creek on an Abra (water taxi). Walk through Gold & Spice Souks in Deira. Afternoon: Lunch at Al Ustad Special Kebab (authentic Emirati & Persian food). Return to the hotel for some rest. Evening: Desert Safari (4x4 dune bashing, sandboarding, camel rides). Enjoy a BBQ dinner & live entertainment (belly dance, fire show). Return to hotel late at night.
